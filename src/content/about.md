## What Is Aletheia?

**Aletheia** is an original fantasy world developed for tabletop
role‑playing games. This site serves as the canonical reference for the
setting: its history, cultures, locations, assumptions, and internal
logic.

The material here has grown out of decades of play and world‑building.
It is intended to support long‑term campaigns and thoughtful exploration
of the setting, rather than provide a fixed narrative or single "official"
story.

<hr />

## Scope of the Site
The primary focus of this site is **world lore**—information that defines
Aletheia as a setting, independent of any single game or campaign.

Over time, the site will also include:

- Campaign notes from games set in Aletheia  
- Observations and lessons learned through play  
- Practical tips, tools, and links related to running and playing games
  in the setting

Not all content represents the same level of abstraction. World lore is
curated to remain internally consistent, while campaign material may
reflect specific interpretations, timelines, or outcomes.

<hr />

## Game Systems
Aletheia is designed to be **largely system‑agnostic**. The setting is
intended to be adaptable to different tabletop role‑playing systems.

That said, some material—particularly campaign notes and practical
advice—assumes the use of **GURPS**, which is the primary system used in
our games. When system‑specific assumptions are present, they are noted
as such.

## Who Is Behind Aletheia?
This project is maintained by two long‑time tabletop role‑playing gamers and (now) fantasy world‑builders. We have been playing and running RPGs on and off for decades, and Aletheia exists as a way to organize, refine, and share the world that the two of us have had in the back of our minds for many years.

The site itself is self‑built. Coded by me (Brad) and AI. By profession, I am a senior IT professional, which influences how the site is structured and maintained.

My brother also contributes to the setting and related campaign material. He is a fantasy world‑builder and long‑time gamer. Additional details about contributors will be added as the project develops.

<hr />

## Canon and Interpretation
Material published here represents the current canon of the world of Aletheia.

Campaign notes and examples may depict specific events, perspectives, or outcomes that apply only to a particular game. Core world information is curated separately to preserve consistency across campaigns.
